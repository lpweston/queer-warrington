Businesses

Our list of businesses are either people known to or places visited by the members of Queer Coffee. We list Queer owned or run businesses, as well as places known to have staff that have been courteous towards those who do not display normative gender indicators, therefore trans and non-binary people have not been dismissed, ignored or asked to leave. This is why we approve them.

The list is not exclusive! We hope there are many more businesses in town who are willing to engage with us in a respectful manner.
