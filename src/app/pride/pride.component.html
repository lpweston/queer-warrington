Warrington Pride 2025

14th June 2025

During times when our rights are being challenged and taken away, it's more important than ever that we should show our pride visibly! Pride is a time when we can come together in love, support, celebration and defiance.
There are lots of events across the town for everyone to enjoy, which we have collected here for you. If you know of any other events that should be listed here please don't hesitate to reach out to us at warrqueercoffee@gmail.com

Where is Warrington Pride?

There are activities going on throughout the town!

  • There are two stages entertaining you all day: one at Time Square and the other in Golden Square's Old Market Place
  • Funfair, workshops and stalls at Golden Square
  • Talks, activities and crafts inside the University of Chester
  • Family fun at Warrington Youth Zone and the Library
  • And evening entertainment at the Geek Retreat and Parr Hall.
