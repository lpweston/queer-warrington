Where is Warrington Pride?

There are activities going on throughout the town!

  • Golden Square has a stage entertaining people all afternoon
  • Talks, activities and crafts inside the University of Chester
  • Family fun at the Library
  • And evening entertainment at the Geek Retreat and Parr Hall.

The Parade

Pre-parade speeches start at Time Square at 11:30am, with the parade starting at 12:00pm, and will wind it's away over the Skittles, down Sankey street, into and around the Golden Square, ending in Old Market Square.

For those who might have difficulty navigating through the Golden Square an alterative route is available moving directly from the Skittles to Old Market Square.
